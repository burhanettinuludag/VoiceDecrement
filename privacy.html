Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Summary: VoiceDecrement processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or voice recordings to external servers.

Data Collection

VoiceDecrement does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

Voice Recordings

Voice recordings made within the app are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Fully under your control to delete at any time

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Storage

All app data, including profiles and test results, is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

VoiceDecrement does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
