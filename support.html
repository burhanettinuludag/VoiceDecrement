Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a voice test?

Create a profile, then tap "New Test Session". Follow the on-screen instructions to record sustained "aaa" sounds for each trial.

How many trials should I record?

We recommend recording 10 trials per session for meaningful results. You can see the change patterns across trials in the results view.

What does the Change Index show?

The Change Index measures how your voice acoustic characteristics (amplitude, frequency) change across trials compared to your baseline recordings.

Is my data private?

Yes. All recordings and data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual profiles or test sessions from within the app. To remove all data, simply delete the app from your device.

The microphone isn't working

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and ensure VoiceDecrement has permission enabled.

About

VoiceDecrement is designed to help users track voice changes during extended vocal use. It's perfect for teachers, singers, public speakers, and anyone interested in understanding their voice patterns.

Note: This app is for personal voice tracking and educational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses or medical advice.
